Riverhaven is a small cooperative community in Soldotna, Alaska offering a new model of independent living—one rooted in personal freedom, shared values, and a deeper connection to nature.

Inspired by Blue Zone longevity principles, Riverhaven promotes wellness through walkability, simplicity, and neighborly support—not obligation. Whether you're seeking a place to retire actively, work remotely, or invest in a slower pace of life, Riverhaven offers a nurturing environment that prioritizes health, sustainability, and community by choice.
