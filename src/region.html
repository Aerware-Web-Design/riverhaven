The Region

The Riverhaven property is set within a secluded subdivision surrounded by 210 acres of protected parkland, offering residents an immersive connection to nature. The land includes well-maintained walking trails that wind along the river and through majestic old-growth forests, providing ample opportunities for outdoor activities, reflection, and exercise—key components of a healthy, longevity-focused life.

Why Riverhaven and Soldotna?

  • Soldotna is a thriving community that offers the perfect balance of natural beauty and local charm.
  • With its breathtaking scenery, the Kenai River provides endless opportunities for fishing, hiking, and outdoor recreation.
  • Living in Riverhaven means you’re part of a quiet, close-knit community, surrounded by the vast wilderness of Alaska, yet connected to the practices that encourage a long, fulfilling life.
